5. Data Retention보관 기간

  • Account & subscription data: kept while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days after account deletion, except where retention is legally required (e.g., tax records related to payments).
  • Chat history (browser-side, IndexedDB): stored locally in your browser until you clear browser data or delete your account.
  • Chat content sent to AI providers: retention governed by the provider's terms (Google Gemini's default at the time of writing).
  • Server logs: typically 30–90 days.
  • Payment records: retained for the period required by applicable tax law (typically 5–7 years).
  • 계정 및 구독 정보: 계정이 활성인 동안 보관. 계정 삭제 후 30일 이내 파기 (단, 결제 관련 세무 기록 등 법령상 보관 의무가 있는 정보 제외).

  • AI 제공자 전송 내용: 해당 제공자 약관 (작성일 기준 Google Gemini 기본 정책)에 따름.

  • HTTPS / TLS for all traffic in production.
  • Session cookies are signed (HMAC-SHA256) and httpOnly.
  • OAuth flows use state-cookie CSRF protection.
  • Stripe webhook signatures are verified before any state change.
  • Payment card data never touches our servers.
  • 운영 환경 전체 트래픽 HTTPS / TLS.
  • 세션 cookie 는 서명(HMAC-SHA256) + httpOnly.
  • OAuth 흐름에 state cookie CSRF 방지 적용.
  • Stripe webhook 서명 검증 후 상태 변경.
  • 카드 정보는 저희 서버를 거치지 않습니다.

No system is perfectly secure. We encourage you to use a unique, strong password on your Google account and enable 2-factor authentication.
